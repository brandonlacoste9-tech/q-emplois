# q-emplois Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Name**: Cuir Québécois (Québec Leather)
- **Vibe**: Warm, premium, trustworthy, local
- **Primary aesthetic**: Dark navy + warm gold + cream, with leather textures

## Core Colors (from design-tokens.ts)
- `gold`       → #D9A441 (accent, buttons, highlights)
- `cream-hi`   → #F5EDE3 (headings, important text)
- `navy`       → #13293D (backgrounds, dark sections)
- `success`    → #7FB069 (RBQ badges, positive states)
- `danger`     → #C46B6B (errors, warnings)

## Typography
- Headings: `serif` (elegant, classic)
- Body: `body-f` (clean, readable)
- Accents: Small caps + letter-spacing on nav items

## Key Components
- `.stitch-box`: Card with stitched/leather feel
- `.gold-btn`: Primary action button
- `.ghost-btn`: Secondary / subtle actions
- `AppShell`: Consistent navbar + bottom nav on mobile

## Rules
- Always use the design tokens from `frontend/src/styles/design-tokens.ts`
- Keep French-first typography and tone
- Maintain high contrast for accessibility
- Prefer warm, premium textures over flat modern design

## Files to Reference
- `frontend/src/styles/design-tokens.ts`
- `frontend/src/styles/cuir-quebecois.css`
- `frontend/src/styles/theme.css`

## Signature Style: Tanned Leather + Stitching

This is the heart of the visual identity. Prioritize these elements:

- **Tanned Leather**: Warm, slightly textured, rich brown/gold tones with depth. Avoid flat or overly modern surfaces.
- **Stitching**: Visible dashed or dotted borders (`.stitch-box`) that give a handcrafted, premium feel.
- **Color Palette**: Deep navy backgrounds + warm gold accents + cream text on leather-like cards.
- **Material Feel**: Use subtle shadows, borders, and textures that evoke high-quality leather goods.

### Do's
- Use `.stitch-box` for cards and containers
- Keep gold (#D9A441) as the main accent
- Maintain warm, slightly vintage premium tone

### Don'ts
- Avoid cold grays, neon colors, or glassmorphism
- Do not remove stitching effects
- Avoid overly flat or minimalist redesigns

This style should remain consistent across all future pages and features.
